Cam-2-Cam

Cam-2-Cam explores dual-camera interaction techniques for smartphone-based augmented reality, enabling users to fluidly transition between front- and rear-facing camera views as part of a unified spatial interaction.

Read the full publication here: https://dl.acm.org/doi/10.1145/3694907.3765919
